# Task1- Robitoc Dog

## Description
intial design for a robotic dog.

## Tools
- Tinkercad

## Mechanichal Design Summary

- **Number of Joints:** 8.
- **Degree of Freedom:** 8.
- **Motors:** Servo Motors.
- **Estimated Torque:** Approximately **0.2 N*m**
- **Stability:** The center of gravity is located near the middle of yhe body, with the weight evenly distributed across all four legs to stability.
- **Walking Method:** Alternating diagonal gait, where the front right leg moves together with the rear left leg, followed by the front left leg and the rear right leg.
- **Expected Mechanical Issues:** Insufficent motor torque, joint friction, and leg slippage on smooth surfaces.
